Privacy & Cookie Policy

Effective date: 7 Nov 2025

1. Who we are

Golden Connections Community Center (the “Center”, “we”, “our”, “us”) operates the website https://www.goldenconnections.org. Our mission is to provide social, emotional and intellectual programs for adults 60 + in York County, PA.

2. What personal data we collect

Name, email, phoneProvided voluntarily when you use the Contact Us form.
IP address & browser informationCollected automatically by the web server for security and analytics.
Cookie identifiersSee the Cookie Policy for details.

We never collect health data, financial data, or any other sensitive personal information.

3. Why we process your data

  • Contact requests – to reply to questions, schedule visits or send program information.
  • Newsletter subscription – only if you explicitly opt‑in.
  • Website analytics – aggregated, anonymous data to improve the site experience.
  • Legal compliance – we retain contact‑form records for up to 12 months to meet record‑keeping requirements.

4. How long we keep your data

  • Contact‑form submissions: ≤ 12 months.
  • Newsletter mailing list: until you unsubscribe.
  • Log files (IP, browser): automatically deleted after 30 days.

5. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Ask for a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal limits).
  • Withdraw consent for non‑essential cookies at any time.
  • Object to direct marketing (newsletter) messages.

To exercise any of these rights, please email privacy@goldenconnections.org.

6. How we protect your data

  • All data is stored on encrypted servers with restricted access.
  • Transport is secured with HTTPS (TLS 1.3).
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability scans are performed.

7. Cookies & tracking

We use both essential and non‑essential cookies. Details are explained in our Cookie Policy. You can manage your preferences through the cookie‑consent banner that appears on your first visit.

8. Third‑party links

Our site may contain links to external resources (e.g., Google Maps). Those sites have their own privacy policies; we are not responsible for their practices.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Effective date” at the top reflects the latest revision. Continuing to use the site after a change indicates your acceptance of the updated terms.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@goldenconnections.org.
