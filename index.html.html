What We Teach

Practical skills for staying connected, safe, and independent

📱 Smartphones & Basics

Start with the fundamentals: Making calls, texting, taking photos, and using essential apps with confidence

💻 Computer Skills

Master the essentials: Email, internet browsing, file management, and basic computer operations

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Staying Connected

Connect with family: Video calls with family, social media basics, and photo sharing made simple

🔐 Password Safety

Stay secure: Creating strong passwords, using password managers, and keeping accounts secure

🛡️ Scam Protection

Stay safe online: Recognizing scams, avoiding social engineering, and protecting yourself online

🏥 Healthcare Technology

Manage your health: Patient portals, virtual doctor visits, and managing your health online

🤖 AI & Modern Tech

Understand new technology: AI assistants, smart home devices, and emerging technologies explained simply

🛒 Online Services

Shop and bank safely: Safe online shopping, banking basics, and using helpful websites

Important: What We Focus On

Golden Connection focuses on education and training, not technical repair services.

We don't provide device repair, software installation, troubleshooting, or hands-on tech support. Our mission is to teach you the skills and confidence to use technology independently.

Meet Blu, Your Tech Helper

Your Friendly Tech Companion

Meet Blu, our gentle blue hare mascot who embodies the patience and understanding that makes learning technology feel less intimidating. Just like our instructors, Blu is here to make your tech journey feel like connecting with a trusted friend!

Why a blue hare? Blue represents trust and calm, while the gentle hare symbolizes patience and careful listening - exactly what you'll find in our classes.
