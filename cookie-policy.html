Cookie Policy

Effective date: November 7, 2025

Last updated: November 7, 2025

Simple Summary: Golden Connection does not use cookies on this website. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies of any kind.

1. No Cookies in Use

Some websites store small text files called "cookies" on your device to remember your preferences, track usage, or show advertising. Golden Connection currently does none of this.

We do not set any cookies when you visit our website. You can browse the entire site without any tracking, analytics, or advertising technologies that rely on cookies.

2. Future Changes

If we ever decide to use cookies in the future (for example, simple visitor analytics), we will:

  • Update this Cookie Policy before any cookies are enabled
  • Explain exactly what types of cookies we use and why
  • Give you a clear choice to accept or decline non-essential cookies
