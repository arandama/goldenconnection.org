Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 7, 2025

Last updated: November 7, 2025

Simple Summary: Golden Connection collects only the basic information you choose to share when you contact us (like your name, email, and general location). We do not use cookies, we do not run analytics or advertising trackers, and we never sell your data.

1. Who We Are

Golden Connection provides free technology education to adults (primarily 55+) in smaller communities across the United States, with a focus on rural areas.

Organization Status: 501(c)(3) nonprofit status pending. Federal tax-exempt application has been filed with the IRS and is currently under review.

Service Area: Nationwide (we travel to communities to provide in-person education)

Contact:

2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you contact us through our website contact form or email, we may collect:

Information Type What We Collect Why We Need It
Name Your first name (or full name if you provide it) To address you personally in our response
Email Address Your email address To respond to your inquiry or service request
General Location City/county or general area (e.g., "rural Pennsylvania") To determine if we can serve your area and for high-level impact reporting
Message Content Whatever you write in the contact form or email To understand and respond to your questions or needs

What we DON'T collect: We do not ask for or intentionally collect Social Security numbers, financial information, health data, precise GPS location, passwords, or other sensitive personal information. Please do not include this kind of information in your messages to us.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Our hosting provider may generate basic server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited) to keep the website secure and running properly. Golden Connection does not link this technical data to individual identities and does not use it for tracking or profiling.

We do not use Google Analytics or any other analytics, advertising, or social media tracking tools.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Golden Connection does not use cookies of any kind on this website. We do not set essential, analytics, or advertising cookies, and we do not use pixel tags or similar tracking technologies.

If we decide to add simple analytics in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and our Cookie Policy before anything is enabled, and give you a clear choice.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited personal information we collect only to:

  • Respond to your questions or requests
  • Determine whether we can provide services in your area
  • Plan and improve our programs in a general, non-identifying way

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with anyone. We also do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Legal Basis and Data Sharing

We process your information based on your consent (when you choose to contact us) and our legitimate interest in providing and improving our nonprofit educational services.

We may share information only when required by law (for example, in response to a valid legal request) or with service providers who help us operate email or hosting, under agreements that protect your data.

6. Data Retention

We keep your messages and contact details only as long as needed to respond to you and to keep a basic record of our outreach. We review stored communications periodically and delete information that is no longer needed.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights such as:

  • Requesting a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Asking us to correct inaccurate information
  • Asking us to delete your information where appropriate

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@goldenconnection.org. We are a small nonprofit, but we will do our best to respond promptly and transparently.

8. Children’s Privacy

Golden Connection’s services are designed for adults, primarily age 55 and older. Our website and programs are not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services or the law. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If the changes are significant, we may also post a notice on our website.
